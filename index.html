Booking & Payment Policies

Booking is very simple! You can click any of the WhatsApp buttons on our website. This will direct you to a WhatsApp chat with us, pre-filled with your enquiry. We will confirm availability and secure your booking immediately.

We accept bank transfers (direct deposit) and cash payments. You can pay via bank transfer to confirm the booking or pay in cash at the start/end of the tour.

No, you do not need to pay the full amount in advance. To secure and guarantee your booking, a reasonable advance payment is sufficient. The remaining balance can be settled later.

We understand that travel plans can change. If you have to cancel your tour for any reason, we offer a flexible cancellation policy with a 100% refund of your advance payment.

For multi-day and overnight hires, driver's bata (allowances) and accommodation charges are calculated on a per-night basis depending on the destination. Please discuss this with us when booking to get a transparent breakdown.
